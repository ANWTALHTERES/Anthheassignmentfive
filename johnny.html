Johnny Rain

Unlike other artist I find that Johnny is more into the music for the music and not the fame, also the reason why a lack of pictures and info is on him.

Born on September 12, 1993 (age 28) in Murrieta, California, United States. He is a celebrity singer. His genres are R&B/Soul, Pop. Hip hop artist who rose to prominence thanks to his gentle and ethereal sound combined with a classic dance beat on SoundCloud. In January of 2015, he released his debut studio album 11 on iTunes.
